About me

Bianca I. Colón-Rosado

After 7 incredible years, I was recently part of a layoff at Taskrabbit.

This was my first job out of college — I joined as a Junior Engineer and grew into a Senior DevOps Engineer making real impact. Over the years, I helped manage and scale our cloud infrastructure with minimal downtime, strengthened the company’s cloud and application security, and supported new integrations after the Dolly acquisition.

Taskrabbit taught me to move fast, fail and recover quickly, take ownership, and create impact even with limited resources. I’m endlessly grateful for the opportunity to collaborate with so many talented and passionate people — and for the trust placed in me along the way.

As Romans 8:28 reminds me, “all things work together for good.” I’m now looking for my next opportunity where I can continue to build, automate, and scale systems that make a difference.

I’ll continue to be Strong, Fierce, Bold, and Kind, as my former mentor taught me — and I’ll keep making everything that surrounds me better.

If you know of DevOps or Cloud Engineering roles (AWS, Terraform, CI/CD, etc.), I’d love to connect.
